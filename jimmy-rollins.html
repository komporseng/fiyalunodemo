    Biography

    Jimmy Rollins was born in Oakland, CA on November 27, 1978. Shortstop who helped bring a World Series title to the Philadelphia Phillies in 2008. He won 3 straight Gold Glove awards from 2007 to 2009, led the National League in triples four times between 2001 and 2007, and was named 2007 NL MVP. Prior to the 2015 MLB season, he was traded to the Los Angeles Dodgers. He executed many double plays with second baseman Chase Utley during his time in Philadelphia. Before beginning his baseball career, he appeared in several MC Hammer music videos in the 1980s.
