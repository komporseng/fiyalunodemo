    Remembering Leonard Diggs: A Tribute to a Beloved Frederick Resident

    As we gather here today, we pay tribute to Leonard Diggs, a beloved member of the Frederick community who has left an indelible mark on our hearts. Leonard, or Len as he was affectionately known, was a remarkable individual whose vibrant personality and kind heart touched the lives of many. Today, we come together to remember his extraordinary life and honor the immense contributions he made to our city.

    Leonard Diggs’ Life and Contributions

    Leonard S. “Len” Diggs III was born on December 11, 1992, and sadly passed away on October 24, 2023, at the age of 30. He was the cherished son of Leonard S. Diggs Jr. and Elinore H. Diggs. Len’s thirst for knowledge led him to pursue his education at New Life Christian School, where he graduated in 2011. He furthered his studies at Montgomery College, earning an associate degree, and later obtained his bachelor’s degree from Liberty University.

    Len’s dedication to education extended beyond his own studies. He returned to his alma mater, New Life Christian School, where he served in various important roles, making a positive impact on both staff and students. After completing his studies at Liberty University, Len pursued his passion for broadcasting and joined Key 103.1 in Frederick. As an on-air personality known as “Buster Collins,” he hosted the early afternoon time slot and produced captivating podcasts that entertained and inspired listeners.

    Outside of his professional endeavors, Len had a deep love for professional wrestling and attended numerous events throughout his life. He also recently embarked on a journey to study Judo, demonstrating his commitment to personal growth and self-improvement.

    Len’s legacy lives on through his loving family, including his parents Leonard Sr. and Elinore Diggs, and his brother Stephen Diggs. He is also survived by beloved aunts, uncles, and cousins who will forever cherish the memories they shared with him.

    Service Details and Condolences

    The family will be receiving friends on Thursday, November 2, 2023, from 5:00 pm to 8:00 pm at Stauffer Funeral Home located at 1621 Opossumtown Pike in Frederick. A celebration of Leonard’s remarkable journey through life will take place on Friday, November 3, 2023, at 11:00 am at Frederick Christian Fellowship Church (FCF), located at 10142 Hansonville Road in Frederick. Interment will follow at Resthaven Memorial Gardens.

    In lieu of flowers, the family kindly requests contributions to the GoFundMe page they have established in Leonard’s honor. Expressions of sympathy and condolences can be offered to the family at www.StaufferFuneralHome.com.

    Let us join together in honoring and remembering Leonard Diggs, a beloved member of our community whose vibrant personality and kind heart will be deeply missed by all who knew him.
