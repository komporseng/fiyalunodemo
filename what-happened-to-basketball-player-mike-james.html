    What Happened to Basketball Player Mike James?

    Mike James, a prominent basketball player, has been making waves in the European basketball scene, particularly in the EuroLeague and LNB Pro A.

    His journey from the NBA to European leagues, along with his recent career developments, has been a subject of interest and intrigue among basketball enthusiasts and fans. 

    From NBA to European Leagues

    Mike James made a significant transition from the NBA to European leagues, signing with AS Monaco after departing from his previous team, CSKA Moscow.

    This move marked a spectacular shift for James, as AS Monaco competes against top teams in France and is also a member of the prestigious Euroleague.

    European Career Highlights

    • Mike James has established himself as a top point guard in Europe, showcasing his attacking versatility and defensive prowess.
    • His career in European basketball has taken him to various countries such as Croatia, Israel, Italy, Greece, and Spain, allowing him to develop a strong foundation in the sport.
    • Notably, he has been a game-changing player in the Euroleague, emerging as the top scorer in the 2018-2019 season.

    Criticism and Controversy

    Despite his success in European basketball, Mike James has faced criticism from European coaches, particularly for his perceived lack of mood compatibility, leading to his departure from CSKA Moscow.

    Professional Journey

    Mike James' professional journey spans across different teams and leagues, showcasing his versatility and adaptability as a basketball player.

    NBA Career

    • James initially signed with Croatian club Zagreb for the 2012–13 season before venturing into Israel’s second-tier division with Hapoel Galil Elyon.
    • Subsequently, he joined Paffoni Omegna in Italy and later moved to Kolossos Rodou in Greece.
    • James also had stints in the NBA, signing with the Phoenix Suns for the 2015 NBA Summer League and later re-signing with Baskonia for another season.
    • His NBA career included contracts with the Phoenix Suns, New Orleans Pelicans, and the Brooklyn Nets, where he signed a 10-day contract and was later retained for the remainder of the 2020–21 season.

    European Stint

    • James' successful transition to European basketball involved playing for renowned teams such as Baskonia, Panathinaikos, and CSKA Moscow, where he made significant contributions to their respective leagues and tournaments.

    Personal Details

    • Full Name: Michael Perry James
    • Birth Date: August 18, 1990
    • Nationality: American
    • Teams: Zagreb, Hapoel Galil Elyon, Omegna, Kolossos Rodou, Baskonia, Panathinaikos, Phoenix Suns, New Orleans Pelicans, Brooklyn Nets, AS Monaco Basket
    • Net Worth: $2 million
    • NBA Draft: 2012 / Undrafted
    • Position: Point guard
    • Leagues: LNB Pro A, EuroLeague
    • Social Media: Instagram, Twitter

    Recent Career Developments

    Mike James' recent career developments have been centered around his impressive performance with AS Monaco Basket in the LNB Pro A and the EuroLeague.

    Performance and Achievements

    • In his current EuroLeague season, James averages 19.4 points, 4.3 rebounds, and 5.3 assists, reflecting his significant contributions to the team.
    • His potential to become the all-time leading scorer in EuroLeague history further underscores his impact as a basketball player.
    • Despite his success, James acknowledges the challenges and fatigue associated with being a basketball player, hinting that his next contract might mark the end of his basketball journey.

    Mike James' journey from the NBA to European basketball has been filled with remarkable achievements, challenges, and controversies.

    As he continues to make strides in his career, James remains a compelling figure in the realm of professional basketball, captivating fans with his talent and determination.
