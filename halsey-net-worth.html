    Ashley Nicolette Frangipane Wiki Biography

    Ashley Nicolette Frangipane – better known by her stage name Halsey, an anagram of her Christian name – was born on the 29th September, 1994 in Edison, New Jersey USA, of Irish, Hungarian and Italian ancestry, and is a musician, singer and songwriter, probably best recognized for her single “Closer”, which she released with The Chainsmokers, and her two studio albums – “Badlands” and “Hopeless Fountain Kingdom”. Her musical career has been active since 2014.

    So, have you ever wondered how rich Halsey is, as of early 2018? According to authoritative sources, it has been estimated that the total size of Halsey’s net worth is over $5 million, accumulated through her successful involvement in the music industry.

    Halsey Net Worth $5 Million

    Halsey was raised with two younger brothers in her hometown by her father Chris Frangipane – a car dealer – and mother, Nicole Frangipane, who works in a hospital for a security crew. As a young girl, she played the cello, viola and violin until the age of 14, when she also started playing the acoustic guitar. Upon matriculation, Halsey enrolled into the Rhode Island School of Design, but soon quit her education as she couldn’t afford the tuition, and was also kicked out of home. Thus she started playing the guitar and performing in various night clubs, while living for some time with her grandmother. Parallel with her performances, Halsey started to write songs, and when her song “Ghost” achieved considerable success on SoundCloud, she signed a contract with Astralwerks Records.

    So, Halsey’s professional musical career began in 2014, after a tour with The Kooks band, when she released her debut EP entitled “Room 93”, which peaked at No. 3 on the Top Heatseekers Charts, No. 37 on the Top Alternative Albums chart and No. 159 on the Billboard 200 chart, and which marked the beginning of an increase of her net worth.

    In the following year, Halsey released her debut studio album entitled “Badlands”, which reached No. 2 on the US Billboard 200 chart and was certified platinum by RIAA, and was positively critiqued by Rolling Stone magazine, adding a considerable amount to her net worth,  especially as she co-wrote all songs from the album. During that period, she collaborated with Justin Bieber on his fourth studio album “Purpose”, and in 2016 she started her collaboration with The Chainsmokers on her single “Closer”, which topped the Billboard Hot 100 chart; the single was nominated for Best Pop Duo/Group Performance at the 59th Annual Grammy Awards, increasing her net worth by a large margin.

    To speak further about her career, Halsey released the single “Not Afraid Anymore”, which found its place on the soundtrack for the film “Fifty Shades Darker” (2017). In June of the same year, out came her second studio album entitled “Hopeless Fountain Kingdom”, which reached the top of the Billboard 200 chart with two hit singles – “Now Or Never” and “Bad At Love”. She is currently on the Hopeless Fountain Kingdom World Tour to promote her album – her net worth is certainly still rising.

    When it comes to speak about her personal life, Halsey has been in a relationship with rapper G-Eazy since July of 2017. She previously dated Norwegian music producer Lido (2014-2016) and Ashton Irwin in 2016. At the age of 17, she was diagnosed with bipolar disorder, after which she became addicted to drugs and tried to commit suicide. Nowadays she participates in the campaign against suicide. In her spare time, Halsey is active on her official Instagram account.
