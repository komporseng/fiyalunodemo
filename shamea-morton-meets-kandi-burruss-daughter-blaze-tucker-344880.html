    Shamea Morton had the chance to meet Kandi Burruss and Todd Tucker’s daughter, Blaze Tucker. As you probably know by now, Kandi had a surrogate, and now they are all celebrating the latest addition to the family.

    Ace Wells Tucker, Kaela Tucker, and Riley Burruss have a little sister now, and fans cannot be more excited.

    Shamea shared the following post on her social media account after meeting the baby girl.

    ‘🎀A BEAUTIFUL Blaze went through the Hospital yesterday! 💖I was fortunate enough to meet @blazetucker today, and she’s everything I imagined and more. 😍 Her name fits her perfectly. Congratulations @kandi and @todd167 Sidenote….it was funny how possessive @acetucker and @rileyburruss were of their baby sis. They def don’t have to worry about a baby sitter bc they had it on lock. 😍 PS…it was my idea for her to lay in bed!😂 CALM DOWN,’ Shamea captioned her post.

    Kandi offered her gratitude to Shamea and commented: ‘Thanks for stopping by & bringing all those gifts for @blazetucker! 😘’
