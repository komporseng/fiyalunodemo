    In 2005, Alexis DeJoria began her NHRA career competing in the Super Gas category. It was before moving into a Super Comp dragster.

    Similarly, she won her 1st ever NHRA national event, at the 2011 NHRA Northwest Fall Nationals. Therefore, she also became the second woman ever to do so.
